Data, codes and preprints

I strongly advocate for an open and reproducible research model and culture that may ultimate contribute to a more accessible, transparent and reliable science. This led me to co-found Open Plant Pathology Initiative. Here in the Lab we are users and developers of R language and Tidyverse for data science. All data and computational codes produced during our researchare made available ahead of peer-review. You can find our code on GitHub. The final outcomes such as research compendium and preprints are archived in Open Science Framework (OSF).
